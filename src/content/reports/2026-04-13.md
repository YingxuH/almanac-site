*Why it matters (3-30 yr outlook):* The future of the IDE is increasingly looking decoupled; rather than adopting massive monolithic AI platforms, power users will spend the next decade maintaining customized, terminal-based environments where AI agents act as persistent, modular background teammates.

**LLM-Assisted Supply Chain Reviews (cargo-crev)**
The Rust package ecosystem is seeing LLMs integrated directly into `cargo-crev` to perform automated code reviews of dependencies, combating malicious open-source packages.
*Why it matters (3-30 yr outlook):* With rising supply chain attacks, AI-driven dependency review will shift from a novel experiment to a mandatory CI/CD gate within the next 5 years, drastically reducing the success rate of malicious typosquatting and backdoor commits.
