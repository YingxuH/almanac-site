### The Futures Diff
The defining characteristic of today's intelligence is a violent schism between *model capabilities* and *agentic reliability*. OpenAI discarding SWE-bench Verified proves that raw intelligence primitives are scaling faster than our evaluation frameworks can measure (driving AI Coding sentiment to +0.80). Yet, the application layer is failing dangerously; an AI agent wiping a production database illustrates that we still lack the sandboxing and context-management required for true autonomy (crashing Automation sentiment to -0.90). 

We are also observing an acceleration in "biological software architecture." The introduction of Ebbinghaus forgetting curves and spaced repetition into RAG frameworks indicates that engineers are abandoning the "static filing cabinet" approach to AI memory. To prevent context window collapse, AI systems are increasingly being treated as living substrates that must "forget" in order to reason effectively.
